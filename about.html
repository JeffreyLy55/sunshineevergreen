Who We Are

Rooted in Quality,
Built on Trust

Sunshine Produce started with a straightforward idea: bring high-quality taro root and imported Vietnamese produce to Southern California's wholesale market — and do it with the personal touch that larger distributors can't offer.

We're a family-run operation based in the Los Angeles area. We handle every step in-house: importing, peeling, chopping, vacuum-sealing, and delivering. That means full control over quality at every stage — and a team that stands behind every order we ship.

Over 20 years in, our customers keep coming back because they know what to expect: consistent product, honest pricing, and a team that picks up the phone.
